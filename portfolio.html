Weeks 10-11

Unsupervised learning: Clustering by firms' stock prices (2017-2022)

Link to Colab code, here.

The dendrogram displays clusters of stock prices between 2017 and 2022 for eight eight companies in various industries. The hypothesis tested is whether similar industries will be clustered into similar sets of groups or branches. The lower three firms, Swedbank, Mitsubishi and HSBC formed one cluster, where two belong in the financial sector. In the upper cluster, there are two smaller groups, where one branch consists of HM and Spotify. This makes sense as fashion and music are more associated with each other compared to technology-related industries mostly found in the other cluster that consists of Daikin, Samsung and Fast Retailing Co.
